The designs (below) were part of a recruitment process for a home staging company. Home staging involves preparing a property to the highest quality for sale.

I was tasked with creating a logo design and two instagram posts for a particular event. I managed to create a few variations which can be seen below. I used the Avenir and Rockness font, and the HEX colours: #1C1C36 (nabvy blue), #BBA15A (Gold), #FFFFFF (White), #4D4D4D (grey). All of which were part of the brand guidelines.
