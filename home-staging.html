The logo designs (below) were created for Home Staging Association UK & Ireland (HSA). Home Staging is a growing industry worldwide that brings together many different areas of expertise and professionals, such as real estate agents, interior designers, architects, retail professionals, and many others.

The Home Staging Association UK & Ireland connects professionals, suppliers and partners, promoting all related businesses whilst supporting Home Stagers and keeping the industry relevant and strong.

I was tasked with creating a new logo design for one of their awards, the HSA Awards 2022. I managed to create a few variations which can be seen below. I used the Avenir font, and the HEX colours: #1C1C36 (navy blue), #BBA15A (Gold), and #FFFFFF (White). All of which were part of the brand guidelines. Initially, the house icon had sharp edges but I decided to go with softer edges as the client stated it looked a little stiff. They also wanted something very simple and modern so I used a subtle gradient to make it more dynamic and the negative space to create a "H".
