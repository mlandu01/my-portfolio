Social Media Posts

Graphic Design

The social media designs (below) were created for Home Staging Association UK & Ireland (HSA). Home Staging is a growing industry worldwide that brings together many different areas of expertise and professionals, such as real estate agents, interior designers, architects, retail professionals, and many others.

The Home Staging Association UK & Ireland connects professionals, suppliers and partners, promoting all related businesses whilst supporting Home Stagers and keeping the industry relevant and strong.

I was tasked with creating social media Instagram posts and stories for the company's business courses. I also had the opportunity to come up with a varity of logo design ideas which can be seen below. The layout designs were created using Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Xd. The brand guidelines consisted of the fonts Avenir and Rockwell and the HEX colours: #1C1C36 (navy blue), #BBA15A (Gold), and #FFFFFF (White). So, I created a few variations for the requested social media material.

I really like creating social media posts so therefore I had a lot of fun designing the material!
